Using dao architecture, we created a fantasy sports game that simulates a stock market

Jackpot removes the bounds of rosters, drafts, salary caps, and rules of existing fantasy games. Instead, we introduce an intuitive system consisting of shares, dividends, and an exchange

Objective:

Create a portfolio of the best fantasy performers of the season for the highest payout

About Us

Jackpot Fantasy is a new sports betting model set to address the current market inefficiencies. We use a proprietary system built on the Flow blockchain to provide users with a seamless introduction to the ROY ecosystem.

How we got started

Peter and Cole met for the first time in elementary school in their gifted classes. They became closer friends in middle school through an art class and have since journeyed to high school and college together.

Their ventures include video gaming, varsity lacrosse, managing a catering shop, producing music, traveling, e-commerce, competing in hackathons, day trading, intramural basketball, and Jackpot. The two currently reside in Atlanta as roommates and partners in Jackpot as they finish their education at Georgia Tech.
